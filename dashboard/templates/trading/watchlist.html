Watchlist is built automatically. After each daily screen (5:30pm AEST), stocks passing 6+ of 8 AstraTrade trend criteria are added here automatically. They auto-upgrade to a Signal only once the VCP pattern itself completes (3+ tightening contractions with volume dry-up) — the "Rules X/Y passed" badge on each card counts trend + fundamental + VCP rules together and can look high (e.g. 12/16) while VCP is still "—" (not detected); that VCP gate, not the overall rule count, is what actually triggers auto-promotion. Clicking Promote manually skips that gate — it creates a Signal using today's close as the entry pivot (not the VCP pivot shown in a card's Full Metrics panel), and even then no order is placed until a live breakout with volume is confirmed intraday. Use labels to group and organise stocks — the coloured filter chips below let you filter by group instantly.
