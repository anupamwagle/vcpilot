🔍 Layer 2 · AstraTrade Screener (exchange-aware)
Trend Template (8 criteria)Price > 50MA > 150MA > 200MA. 200MA rising ≥21 days. Within 25% of 52-wk high, ≥30% above 52-wk low.
Relative StrengthRS percentile within same-exchange universe. Exchange-scoped — ASX stocks vs ASX peers, US vs US peers.
FundamentalsEPS growth ≥25% (2+ qtrs), sales growth, ROE, margins, inst. ownership. Skipped for crypto assets.
VCP Pattern3–4 successive price/volume contractions. Volume dry-up. Pivot buy point identified. Base length 3–52 weeks.
Market RegimePer-exchange BULL/CAUTION/BEAR. ASX: ^AXJO. US: ^GSPC or ^IXIC. Crypto: BTC-USD as proxy. Breadth + distribution days (equity only).
Volume ConfirmBreakout on ≥2× average 50-day volume. Checked intraday every 5 min during exchange hours.

🛡️ Layer 4 · Risk & Position Sizing (currency-aware)
2% Rule (AUD base)
Max 2% of AUD capital at risk per trade. Capital converted to native currency (USD for US) via live FX rate (AUDUSD=X, Redis cached 1hr).
Stop Loss
Hard stop at VCP pivot low. Auto bracket order on IBKR (or ccxt emulated stop for crypto). Mandatory rule — cannot be disabled.
FX Normalisation
All P&L, risk, and portfolio heat aggregated in AUD. USD positions converted at entry/exit FX rates. FX exposure visible in position data.
Portfolio Heat
Max 15% of AUD capital at risk across all open positions. Blocks new entries when exceeded. Cross-exchange heat computed in AUD.
