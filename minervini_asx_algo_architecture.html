🛡️ Layer 4 · Risk & Position Sizing
1-2% Rule
Max 2% of total capital at risk per trade. With $1k: ~$20 max loss/trade.
Stop Loss
Hard stop at pivot low / 7–10% below entry. Auto bracket order on IBKR.
Position Size
Size = (Capital × Risk%) ÷ (Entry − Stop). Auto-calculated per signal.
Portfolio Limit
Max 3–5 open positions. No single stock >30% of capital. Pyramid only on winners.
